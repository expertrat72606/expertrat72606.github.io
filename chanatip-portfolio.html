My Blog

Learning Game Development: Challenges and Solutions

January 15, 2025

Game development has been one of the most exciting yet challenging parts of my journey as a Unity developer. During my internship at Varisoft, I was introduced to real-world development tasks, which pushed me to grow quickly. At first, I was assigned to a large multiplayer project, but due to its complexity, I was given the chance to create my own hyper-casual games instead.

Working on some games taught me a lot—from writing clean, organized code to designing fun gameplay mechanics. One of my biggest challenges was learning new systems from scratch and structuring my scripts in a way that made them scalable and reusable. With guidance from senior developers and lots of trial and error, I started to understand best practices and improve my workflow.

This experience showed me that game development is a mix of creativity and problem-solving. The key is to keep learning, stay curious, and not be afraid to ask for help when you’re stuck. Every challenge I faced helped me become a better developer, and I’m excited to keep building and improving from here.
